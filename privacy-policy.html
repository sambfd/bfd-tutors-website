Privacy Policy

How BFD Tutors collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information.

1. Who We Are

BFD Tutors is a trading name of EffDee Development Limited, a company registered in England and Wales. For privacy questions or data requests, contact sam@bfdtutors.com.

2. Information We Collect

  • Contact details, such as name and email address.
  • Booking and payment records.
  • Information you choose to share about essays, deadlines, topics, academic goals, or tutoring needs.
  • Session notes, feedback notes, and private action summaries created to support tutoring.

3. How We Use Information

  • To arrange and deliver tutoring sessions.
  • To provide essay planning, feedback, and academic support.
  • To manage invoices, records, and business administration.
  • To respond to enquiries and provide requested resources.
  • To improve student support through private notes and summaries.

4. AI-Assisted Summaries

BFD Tutors may use internal AI tools to generate private meeting summaries and action items. These are used only to improve student support and are treated as confidential tutoring records. Students and clients may not use automated recording, transcription, or AI-capture tools in sessions without prior written consent.

5. Legal Basis

We process personal information where it is necessary to deliver tutoring services, manage a client relationship, comply with legal and accounting duties, or pursue legitimate business interests in providing and improving educational support.

6. Retention

  • Financial and contract records are retained for 6 years for HMRC and business compliance.
  • Standard academic, contact, and tutoring support data is normally deleted 12 months after the tutoring relationship ends.

7. Sharing Information

We do not sell personal information. Information may be processed by trusted service providers used for scheduling, email, invoicing, file storage, or internal productivity, where this is necessary to run the service.

8. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you may have the right to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, or portability of your personal data, and to object to certain processing. To make a request, email sam@bfdtutors.com.

9. Updates

This policy may be updated as the business develops. The current version was prepared on 18/06/2026.
